Vegetarian Butternut Squash Lasagna

Ingredients:

3 3-pound squash, washed and cut into 1/2 inch cubes
2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
Large crystal sea salt and freshly ground pepper
1/2 cup butter
teaspoon of nutmeg
1/2 cup whole wheat or quinoa flour
2 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
4 cups goat milk
3/4 cup (lightly packed) fresh basil leaves
20 no-boil lasagna noodles
cup grated Parmesan

Instructions:

step 1- Heat oil large pan over low heat. step 2- Shake salt and pepper over pan. step 3- Add squash cubes. 
step 4- Splash water into pan then sautee until squash is tender, about 30 minutes. step 5- Smoosh butternut squash cubes in blender, or food processor or with hands. step 6- Season squash mush with additional salt and pepper.
